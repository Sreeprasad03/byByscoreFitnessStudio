Who We Are

BUILT FOR CHAMPIONS

Byscore Fitness Studio is Palakkad's premier fitness destination, founded with a singular mission: to provide world-class training in an environment that pushes you beyond your limits.

Located in Ayyapuram, Palakkad, we bring together elite coaching, modern equipment, and a community of driven individuals. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned athlete, Byscore is your home.

We're also home to Kerala's Pro Arm Wrestling Hub — a specialized facility offering professional arm wrestling training alongside expert rehabilitation for ligament and tendon injuries.
